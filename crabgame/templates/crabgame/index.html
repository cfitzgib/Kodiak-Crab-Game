Contact Us

We are a group of information systems students in Carnegie Mellon University and we are working on an IS consulting project. Our clients are the Kodiak School District and the Kodiak Research Laboratory. Our goal is to find a more efficient way to help scientists identify information of crab oocytes like its area and also harness the power of crowdsourcing by developing a game where students can contribute to analysis process by playing the game and also gain educational rewards in the process.
