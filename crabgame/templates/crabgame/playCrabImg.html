How to Play:

Identify and click on all of the good oocytes on the labeled image on the right side. Looking at the original, microscope image on the left, a good oocyte is one that is both dark and mostly circular. Once a good oocyte on the left has been identified, click on the corresponding oocyte on the right side image to mark it.

* There can be anywere between 0-8 good oocytes in one image

* If 2 oocytes touching are the same color, they are connected and are NOT good oocytes
