How to Play:

On the left, you will see an original image of a crab's oocytes taken by a microscope at the KFRC. On the right, you will see the same original image that has all of its identified oocytes labeled color.

The purpose of the game is to identify and click on all of the good oocytes on the right side, labeled image. Looking at the image on the left, a "good" oocyte is one that is both dark and circular. Once a good oocyte on the left has been identified, click on the corresponding oocyte on the right side image to mark it.

* There can be anywere between 0-8 good oocytes in one image

* If 2 oocytes touching are the same color, they are considered connected and are not "good" oocytes
